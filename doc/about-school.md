# About the school

[← Wiki home](../README.md)

## Purpose

Sharon Chinese Language School (MA Sharon Chinese Language School) is a **nonprofit** community school. The public website must communicate **who we are**, **why we exist**, and **how we serve families**—not only class registration.

This page defines content for **About** and related trust pages. Design should feel **professional**, appropriate for a 501(c) organization, parents, and community partners.

**Our mission**

- Provide Chinese language and culture courses for native and non-native speakers

- Promote mutual understanding through cultural exchange activities
